My Story

The Journey So Far

The Beginning

My fascination with computing began during my undergraduate years, where I ranked #1 in microprocessor programming at the state level. This early success ignited a passion for understanding how machines process information.

The Foundation

At CDAC's Research & Development Center, I worked with tools like Open-MPI and OpenFOAM on Intel MIC co-processors. Achieving 2.5X performance improvements taught me that the best solutions come from deeply understanding the systems you're working with.

The Deep Dive into Data Science

My years at Chistats Labs were transformative. I worked on everything from acoustic signal processing for defense applications to predictive maintenance for industrial assets. These diverse challenges taught me that the best AI solutions solve real problems for real people.

Today: Leading AI Innovation

As Head of AI at Turinton Consulting, I'm building Insights AIa platform that unifies intelligence into a secure, chat-based experience for decision makers. My work combines cutting-edge technologies like LangChain, LangGraph, and advanced RAG pipelines with rigorous engineering practices.
